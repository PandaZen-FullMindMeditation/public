Why I made FullMind

This app came from frustration first, then relief, then a surprise: people started telling me they were using it to fall asleep.

“I was fed up with meditation apps making me feel like I had to do something right. I wanted meditation to feel like I was enjoying my perfect vacation spot.”
Message from the founder

I built FullMind because guided meditation often felt like another task on my list. I didn’t want more instructions. I wanted a space.

Later, in my darker periods, I used this app myself and it genuinely helped me settle. Then testers started telling me they were using it while they slept. That made me understand something bigger: FullMind was not just helping people meditate. It was helping people sleep.

Questions people may ask

Is FullMind a sleep app or a meditation app?

Both, but its emotional center is sleep-first and relief-first. Meditation is the experience language. Calm sleep support is the outcome many people care about most.

Do I have to create my own mix?

No. You can explore community scenes and quick-load options first. The deeper controls are there when you want them, not as a requirement.

Is this medical treatment?

No. FullMind is for general relaxation, stress management, mindfulness, and sleep support. It is not a substitute for medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
