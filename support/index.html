Health advisory

FullMind provides audio, breathing visuals, and immersive relaxation tools for general sleep support, stress management, and mindfulness. It is not a medical app and is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

What FullMind is

FullMind is a sleep-first meditation and relaxation app designed for people dealing with racing thoughts, bedtime overthinking, stress, and calm focus needs. The public social identity currently connected to the app includes Instagram, Threads, and Facebook under the FullMind name and the handle @fullmind.app.
